# Privacy Policy

Simple PDF Merger respects your privacy.

This application runs entirely on the user’s device. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information.

All documents are processed locally on the user’s computer. No files are uploaded to external servers, and no analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

The app does not access the internet unless explicitly initiated by the user (for example, to open a website link).

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please visit:
https://github.com/Hagelkruys/PdfMerger
